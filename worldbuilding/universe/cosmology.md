# LOTU Multiverse Cosmology

## The Multiverse Structure

An expansive multiverse containing countless universes, each with unique physical laws, magical systems, and realities. The LOTU series explores specific universes within this greater multiverse.

## The Ethereal Being

A god-like entity capable of traversing time and space across the entire multiverse, known throughout the worlds but operating from the shadows to guide and assist protagonists across different universes.

## Universe Hierarchy

1. **Multiverse** - The infinite collection of all possible universes
2. **Universe** - A specific reality with consistent physical/metaphysical laws  
3. **World** - Planets or celestial bodies within a universe
4. **Continents/Oceans** - Major landmasses and bodies of water
5. **Cities/Villages/Landmarks** - Specific locations and points of interest

## Novel Focus

Each LOTU novel explores a different universe within this multiverse, following protagonists who encounter the ethereal being's subtle guidance while facing unique challenges in their world.

## Interconnections

- The ethereal being serves as the subtle connecting thread across universes
- Cross-universe references and Easter eggs
- Shared metaphysical principles despite different physical laws
